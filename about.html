About

TLGMS is a passion project developed and maintained by Kurt Justin J. Malubay, a dedicated computer science student and independent game developer. With a strong focus on both gameplay and user experience, TLGMS aims to deliver exciting and creative gaming experiences built from the ground up. Every line of code, every design element, and every feature is crafted with attention to detail and a drive for innovation. This project represents not just a game, but a journey of learning, growth, and creativity powered by one developer's vision and commitment.
